starGAZE

Open Call

starGAZE is a unique professional development initiative to support six emerging LGBTQIA filmmakers.

It will provide a platform for LGBTQIA talent through an internationally recognised film festival with access to Irish and international creatives, with the ambition to increase the number of Irish LGBTQIA stories on screen and showcase the next generation of Irish queer and trans voices. starGAZE will accept applications from LGBTQIA people of all backgrounds, particularly encouraging applicants from the global majority, trans and non-binary people, people from working-class communities, the Travelling community, people with disabilities and all equity-seeking individuals facing additional challenges within the film industry.

Eligibility Criteria

» Have written, produced or directed a short film which has screened at a recognised Irish or inter- national film festival within the last 3 years, or has screened at a number of international LGBTQIA film festivals, or that has been broadcast on an Irish or international network or streaming service.

» Note: If your film has not screened at a recognised festival but has received specific recognition from LGBTQIA communities or platforms we encourage you to reach out to info@gaze.ie for further discussion.

» Or, for writers only: have one piece of full-length theatre work performed professionally in Ireland or one professional radio credit.

» Be working towards a debut feature-length project or television pilot/online commission. » Be an Irish resident.

Aims

» To offer LGBTQIA filmmakers access to senior Irish and international creative talent able to guide emerging filmmakers at crucial points in their careers.

» To raise the profile of LGBTQIA filmmakers by platforming them at the 2024 festival.

» To offer participants a year-long mentorship programme, for consistent and meaningful support from senior LGBTQIA filmmakers.

» To offer participants access to unique talks, screenings and networking events at the festival and year-round.

starGAZE applications will be announced on our website and social media channels in July. Supported by the National Talent Academies.
